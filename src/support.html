Need some help?

Do you want to become a Front End Developer? Are you're stuck? Or you have no idea where to start? Help is on its way! See the support options below.

Join the Discord Community

What's the benefit?

  • It's free, which is always nice.
  • It's a safe environment where you can feel confident asking questions (even one's you may think are silly).
  • Get support from other newbie coders on a similar mission to you.
  • Find out about the best resources and learning tips and tricks so you don't have to spend huge sums of money on a coding bootcamp.
  • Ask me any questions you may have! Anything from where to start, what languages to begin with, how to get a job, etc.

Direct mentorship (limited spots available)

For a limited number of students, I will be providing a more focused mentorship support. This will involve weekly one-to-one video calls. The focus of these calls will be to help structure your learning pathway and ultimately improve your chances of landing a job.

To apply, send an email to james@thefrontendcoach.com outlining a few things:

  • A brief introduction about yourself.
  • Why you want to become a Front End Developer.
  • Your biggest blockers to learning and pursuing your goals at the currrent time.
